Help & Documentation

Setup
  • Connect your Notion, Gmail, and other integrations via the AI chat or dashboard.
  • Use the Manage Notion Sync menu to select which Notion pages/databases to sync.
  • All features require you to be logged in.
Features
  • Chat with FlowFate AI to automate tasks, manage Notion, and more.
  • Notion Page Creation: Click the Notion button in chat to instantly create a new Notion page.
  • Notion Database Query: Type /notion-query <your query> in chat to search your Notion database. Example: /notion-query all tasks due today
  • Notion Link Preview: Paste any Notion page or database link in chat to see its title and a quick preview automatically.
  • Notion Task/Reminder Creation: Type /notion-task <task or reminder> or /remind <reminder> in chat. Example: /remind me to call John tomorrow
  • Recent Notion Updates Feed: Click the Notion feed button or type /notion-feed in chat to see your most recently updated Notion pages and tasks.
  • Two-way Notion sync with robust error handling and retry logic.
  • Mobile-friendly dashboard and chat interface.
  • Automations, reminders, and integration with Gmail, Slack, and more.
Tip: All Notion features require you to connect your Notion account and select which pages/databases to sync in the dashboard.
Troubleshooting
  • If Notion sync fails, check your permissions and that the correct pages/databases are selected.
  • For OAuth issues, try reconnecting the integration via the chat or dashboard.
  • If you see 'permission' or 'unauthorized' errors, ensure you have shared the Notion resource with the integration bot.
  • For persistent issues, try logging out and back in, or clearing your browser cache.
